# The ZeroFlow ROI Financial Framework

Investing in high-ticket technical hardware like V2H systems or automated indoor farms requires more than just a surface-level look at the price tag. You need to understand the **Payback Period**—the moment your investment becomes a profit-generating asset.

## Formula A: V2H & Home Battery Payback

Bidirectional charging (V2H) allows you to use your vehicle as a stationary storage system. The ROI is calculated by comparing the cost of the system against the utility savings and the "saved" cost of buying a standalone battery (like a Tesla Powerwall).

### The Formula
`Payback (Years) = [Net Installation Cost] / [Annual Peak Shaving + Displaced Storage Value]`

*   **Net Installation Cost:** (Bidirectional Charger Cost + Electrical Labor) - (Federal/State Tax Credits).
*   **Annual Peak Shaving:** (Discharge Capacity in kW) x (Utility Peak Rate - Off-Peak Rate) x (Estimated Cycles per Year).
*   **Displaced Storage Value:** In 2026, a 13.5kWh stationary battery costs ~$10,000. If your EV provides 80kWh of storage, the "value" provided is the avoidance of buying multiple stationary units.

## Formula B: AgTech Yield & Food ROI

Automated AgTech systems (Hydroponics/Mycology) provide ROI through the displacement of organic grocery costs.

### The Formula
`Monthly Savings = [Monthly Yield (lbs) x Organic Grocery Cost per lb] - [Nutrients + Electricity]`

*   **Monthly Yield:** A standard 30-plant vertical garden yields ~4-6 lbs of greens/herbs per month.
*   **Organic Grocery Cost:** Use a baseline of $5.00/lb for high-quality organic produce.
*   **Efficiency Constant:** Modern LED arrays use roughly 0.05 kWh per plant per day. In a 30-plant system, that is 1.5 kWh/day or ~45 kWh/month.

## section C: The "Sovereignty Premium"

While harder to quantify, the ZeroFlow ROI model adds a 15% "Sovereignty Premium" to the results. This represents the financial value of **resilience**:
1.  **Grid Independence:** Power during outages.
2.  **Supply Chain Security:** Access to fresh produce regardless of grocery store shortages.
3.  **Data Sovereignty:** Local AI management of these systems without subscription fees.
