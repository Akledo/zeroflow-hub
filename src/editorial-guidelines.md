# Editorial Guidelines

At ZeroFlow, we recognize that our readers depend on us for high-stakes information—from home energy security to medical-grade air filtration. To maintain the highest levels of trust and technical accuracy, all content published on our platform must pass through a rigorous multi-stage review process.

## 1. Technical Accuracy & "Physics-First"
The "Physics-First" principle is the cornerstone of our editorial policy. 
*   **Verification:** All technical claims must be cross-referenced with manufacturer data sheets, engineering whitepapers, or independent bench testing.
*   **Math Check:** Every ROI calculation or performance benchmark is peer-reviewed for mathematical errors and realistic assumptions (e.g., accounting for conversion losses in solar systems).
*   **No Hyperbole:** We avoid buzzwords. If a product claims "infinite" efficiency or revolutionary tech, we dismantle the claim using established scientific principles.

## 2. Testing Methodology
When we perform hands-on testing, we follow standardized protocols:
*   **Environment Control:** Tests are conducted in controlled settings to ensure repeatability.
*   **Equipment:** We use professional-grade sensors (e.g., thermal cameras, power meters, particulate counters) rather than consumer-grade toys.
*   **Long-Term Use:** We prioritize long-term durability tests over "out-of-the-box" impressions.

## 3. Affiliate Disclosure & Independence
ZeroFlow is a reader-supported publication. 
*   **Affiliate Links:** We use affiliate links to earn commissions on products purchased through our site. This funds our research and keeps the site free of intrusive display ads.
*   **Integrity:** Our participation in affiliate programs does not influence our product rankings or technical assessments. We regularly publish "Do Not Buy" warnings for popular products that fail our technical standards.
*   **Transparency:** Every page containing affiliate links clearly displays a disclosure at the top.

## 4. Updates & Corrections
Technology moves fast.
*   **Living Documents:** We periodically review our "Best of" guides to ensure they reflect current hardware versions and software updates.
*   **Corrections:** If an error is found, we issue a clear correction note at the top or bottom of the article. We encourage our readers—many of whom are engineers themselves—to submit technical feedback.

## 5. AI Policy
While we use advanced tools to assist with data organization and research, **no article is ever published without human expert oversight.** Every word is vetted by a writer with domain expertise, and every technical recommendation is verified by a human engineer.

**We don't just write for the internet; we write for the workshop, the home lab, and the off-grid site.**
