# Data Sovereignty for Creatives: Local AI

If you are a writer, researcher, or developer, your intellectual property is your most valuable asset. Using cloud-based AI (ChatGPT, Claude) means your data is processed on servers you don't control. **Local AI** changes the physics of information security.

## 1. Why Local? The Privacy Physics
When you run a model locally, your data never leaves your local area network (LAN). There is no "man-in-the-middle" risk, and your prompts are not used to train future iterations of the model.

## 2. Tools of the Trade: Ollama vs. LM Studio
- **Ollama:** Best for background integration and power users who want a "headless" server.
- **LM Studio:** The most beginner-friendly GUI for writers, providing a ChatGPT-like interface with 100% offline capability.

## 3. Hardware Requirements
To run a GPT-4 class model (like Llama-3 70B) at usable speeds, you need VRAM.
