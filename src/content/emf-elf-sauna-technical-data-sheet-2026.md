## 1. EMF vs. ELF: Understanding the Difference
*   **EMF (Electromagnetic Fields):** Measured in milliGauss (mG). These originate primarily from the heaters themselves.
*   **ELF (Extremely Low Frequency):** Measured in Volts per meter (V/m). These originate from the internal wiring and power supply of the sauna.

**The Prosumer Standard:**
*   EMF: **< 1.0 mG** at the point of contact with the body.
*   ELF: **< 10 V/m** (Most "Low EMF" saunas ignore ELF entirely, which is a major health oversight).
