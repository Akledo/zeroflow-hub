## The Contenders
For whole-home backup, we are looking at the heavy hitters:
- **EcoFlow:** DELTA Pro and Delta 2
- **Jackery:** Explorer 3000 Pro and 2000 Plus

## 1. Capacity and Expansion
A home backup system is only as good as how long it can keep your fridge running.

- **EcoFlow DELTA Pro:** Starts at 3.6kWh but is famously expandable up to a massive 25kWh using Smart Extra Batteries. This makes it a viable solution for multi-day outages.
- **Jackery Explorer 2000 Plus:** Jackery has stepped up their game with the 2000 Plus, which is also expandable. You can link up to 5 add-on batteries to reach 12kWh.
