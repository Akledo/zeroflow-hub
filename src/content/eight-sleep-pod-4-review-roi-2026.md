## Active Cooling vs. Passive Materials

Traditional mattress companies have spent decades marketing "cooling gels" and "breathable foams." In 2026, we know the truth: these are **passive** materials. They can only slow down the rate at which you overheat; they cannot actually remove heat from your body.

The **Eight Sleep Pod 4** utilizes **active** thermal regulation. By circulating water through a specialized "Enclosure," the system can pull heat away from your core or provide warmth during the initial stages of sleep. The Pod 4 can reach temperatures as low as 55°F (13°C) and as high as 110°F (43°C), ensuring you stay in the "Goldilocks zone" regardless of the ambient room temperature.
