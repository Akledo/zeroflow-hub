# The Cloud Exit: NAS ROI Math

Subscription fatigue is real. While $9.99/month for 2TB of cloud storage seems cheap, the 5-year **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** tells a different story.

## 1. The 5-Year TCO Breakdown
- **Google One/iCloud (2TB):** $600 over 5 years.
- **DIY NAS (8TB usable, RAID 1):** $400 (Hardware) + $50 (Electricity) = $450.

At 8TB, the NAS wins by over $1,000 in savings compared to cloud pricing.

## 2. Data Gravity and Privacy
Beyond cost, the NAS offers **Data Sovereignty**. Your photos aren't being scanned by AI models to train their next dataset. By hosting locally, you mitigate the risks of platform lock-in and account bans.

## 3. Hardware Choice: TrueNAS vs. Unraid
[TrueNAS](https://github.com/Akledo/zeroflow-hub/tree/main/content/home-lab-privacy-guide.md) offers the best data integrity via ZFS, while Unraid provides the easiest upgrade path for mixed-size drives.
