# Technical Authority Portfolio

ZeroFlow is a technical resource for prosumers and DIYers focused on high-authority, physics-first engineering solutions in margin-rich niches. We specialize in deep-dive technical analysis of emerging hardware, off-grid energy systems, and high-performance computing.

## Core Technical Pillars

### 1. PCIe P2P Bottleneck Audit
**Focus:** Local AI Infrastructure & Multi-GPU Workstations.
**Content Summary:** A rigorous technical analysis of peer-to-peer (P2P) bandwidth bottlenecks in multi-GPU configurations. 
- **Benchmarks:** Comparative performance data for NVLink vs. PCIe Gen4 and Gen5 scaling.
- **VRAM Pooling:** Documentation of latency and throughput constraints when pooling VRAM across multiple consumer-grade cards (RTX 4090/5090) versus enterprise hardware.
- **Value to Partners:** This content drives high-intent traffic for workstations (Minisforum, Beelink workstations), high-end GPUs, and motherboard vendors.

### 2. Sodium-Ion (Na-Ion) BMS Protocol
**Focus:** Next-Generation Energy Storage & DIY Battery Engineering.
**Content Summary:** A comprehensive guide to configuring Battery Management Systems (BMS) specifically for Sodium-Ion chemistry (e.g., HiNa, CATL cells).
- **Physics-First Approach:** Detailed analysis of charge/discharge curves, thermal management, and cycle-life optimization.
- **Sourcing Audit:** Verified sourcing paths for Grade A cells and technical compatibility matrices for domestic modular batteries (e.g., BigBattery).
- **Value to Partners:** This content targets the most advanced segment of the DIY solar and off-grid market, driving traffic to specialized battery stores (18650BatteryStore) and solar equipment retailers (NAWS, AltE Store).

### 3. Residential Hydrogen Storage Systems
**Focus:** Seasonal Off-Grid Energy & Zero-Emission Backup.
**Content Summary:** Technical feasibility and ROI analysis of AEM electrolyzers and residential fuel cell integration.
- **Components:** In-depth reviews of Enapter EL 4.0 units and HPS Picea seasonal storage stacks.
- **Strategic Value:** Connects high-ticket vendors with architectural and engineering leads for off-grid luxury developments.

### 4. Atmospheric Water Generation (AWG) & Home HBOT Specs
**Focus:** Resource Sovereignty & Longevity Hardware.
**Content Summary:** Technical specification audits for residential water-from-air systems (Watergen) and hard-shell hyperbaric chambers.
- **Authority points:** Comparative analysis of AWG energy efficiency (kWh/Liter) and HBOT safety valve redundancies.
- **Value to Partners:** Positions ZeroFlow as the primary technical validator for $2,500+ biohacking and sovereignty hardware.
