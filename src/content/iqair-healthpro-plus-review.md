## Introduction
The IQAir HealthPro Plus has long been considered the gold standard in air purification. But is it still worth the premium in 2026?

## HyperHEPA Filtration
IQAir's HyperHEPA technology filters particles down to 0.003 microns - significantly smaller than standard HEPA filters. This makes it exceptionally effective at capturing viruses, bacteria, and ultrafine particles.

## Performance
With a CADR of 300+ for smoke, dust, and pollen, the HealthPro Plus handles rooms up to 1,125 square feet. Its V5-Cell MultiGas filter is also excellent at removing VOCs and odors.

## Pros & Cons
- **Pros:** Exceptional filtration, Swiss engineering, long filter life, programmable
- **Cons:** Premium price, bulky design, no smart home integration

## Final Verdict
For those with serious respiratory conditions or who want the absolute best filtration, the IQAir remains unmatched. For most users, more affordable options may suffice.
