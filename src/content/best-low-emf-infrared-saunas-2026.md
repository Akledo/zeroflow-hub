When you sit in an infrared sauna, you are surrounded by heating elements and electrical wiring. High-EMF exposure has been linked to various health concerns, including cellular stress and sleep disruption—the very things many sauna users are trying to solve.

- **Material Quality:** Use of non-toxic, FSC-certified woods (like Western Red Cedar or Eucalyptus).
- **Ease of Assembly:** Can two people put it together in under 90 minutes?
