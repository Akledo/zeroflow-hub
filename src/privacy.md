# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. At ZeroFlow, we champion **data sovereignty**—not just for our hardware recommendations, but for our own digital relationship with you.

## 1. Minimal Data Collection
We collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to operate this site. 
*   **Browsing:** We do not use invasive tracking pixels or fingerprinting techniques. We use privacy-respecting analytics (if any) that prioritize your anonymity.
*   **Newsletter:** If you opt-in to our newsletter, we only collect your email address. We will never sell, lease, or trade your contact information.

## 2. Affiliate and Third-Party Links
ZeroFlow contains links to third-party websites (e.g., Amazon, B&H, specialized vendors). 
*   **Affiliate Cookies:** When you click on an affiliate link, a small cookie may be placed on your browser by the vendor to track your purchase and award us a commission. These cookies are managed by the respective third-party platforms and are subject to their own privacy policies.
*   **External Sites:** We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

## 3. Local-First Principles
We advocate for "local-first" software and hardware. Our site is designed to be lean and accessible, avoiding bloated third-party scripts that compromise your security or browser performance.

## 4. Your Rights
You have the right to:
*   Access any personal information we hold about you (typically just an email address if you subscribed).
*   Request the deletion of your data at any time.
*   Opt-out of any communications by clicking "unsubscribe" in our emails.

## 5. Security
We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect any data transmitted to and from our site.

## 6. Updates
This policy may be updated as we evolve. Any significant changes will be noted here with a "Last Updated" date.

**Last Updated:** June 2026

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at **privacy@ctomail.io**.
