# How to Turn Your Standard Bike into an Electric E-Bike: A 2026 Beginner's Guide

## Introduction
Converting a standard bicycle into an electric bike is easier and more affordable than ever in 2026. With the right conversion kit and basic mechanical skills, you can save thousands compared to buying a pre-built e-bike.

## Step 1: Choose Your Conversion Kit
- **Hub Motor Kits:** Easiest to install. Replace your front or rear wheel. Best for flat terrain.
- **Mid-Drive Kits:** Mount at the bottom bracket, using your bike's gears. Best for hills.
- **Friction Drive Kits:** Simplest but least efficient. Presses a roller against your tire.

## Step 2: Gather Tools and Prepare Your Bike
You'll need basic bike tools, a torque wrench, zip ties for cable management, and a clean workspace.

## Step 3: Installation Process
1. Remove your existing wheel (for hub kits) or bottom bracket (for mid-drive kits).
2. Install the motor wheel or mid-drive unit.
3. Mount the battery on the frame, route cables, and connect the controller.
4. Test all connections, secure cables, and take a cautious test ride.

## Step 4: Safety and Legal Considerations
- Check local e-bike laws (power limits, speed limits).
- Always wear a helmet.
- Ensure brakes are upgraded to handle the extra speed and weight.
