## The Stack: Three Pillars of AI Production

### 1. The Foundation: Descript (The Editor)
[Descript](https://www.descript.com/) changed the game by treating video like a Word document. Instead of looking at waveforms, you edit the transcript. Delete a word in the text, and it's deleted in the video.
- **Role:** Structural editing, "Underdubbing" (correcting mistakes by typing), and automatic filler word removal (uh, um).

### 2. The Voice: ElevenLabs (The Narrator)
[ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/) provides the most human-sounding AI voices in existence. Whether you’re cloning your own voice to save time or using a professional narrator, it eliminates the need for expensive microphones and soundproof booths.
- **Role:** High-fidelity narration, multi-language dubbing, and emotional range control.

### 3. The Visuals: Runway (The Cinematographer)
[Runway](https://runwayml.com/) (specifically Gen-3) allows you to generate cinematic B-roll from text prompts. No more scouring stock footage sites for "man sitting at desk" only to find a clip everyone else has used.
- **Role:** Unique, cinematic B-roll and visual effects that match your script perfectly.

## The Workflow: Step-by-Step Integration

### Step 1: Scripting and "Voice First"
Start by writing your script. Instead of filming yourself, run the script through **ElevenLabs**. 
- **Pro Tip:** Use their "Speech-to-Speech" feature. Record yourself reading the script with the right emotion on your phone, then have ElevenLabs replace your voice with a professional narrator while keeping your original cadence and timing.

### Step 2: The Descript "Paper Edit"
Import the ElevenLabs audio into **Descript**. 
1. Generate the transcript.
2. Use "Overdub" to fix any script errors.
3. Use "Studio Sound" to make the AI voice sound like it was recorded in a multi-million dollar studio.
4. Add your captions/subtitles using their automated templates.

### Step 3: Generating Custom B-Roll with Runway
Now for the visuals. Look at your Descript transcript. For every scene that needs a visual:
1. Copy a sentence from the script.
2. Paste it into **Runway Gen-3** with a style prompt (e.g., "4k, cinematic lighting, 35mm lens").
3. Download the 5–10 second clip and drag it directly into your Descript project.

### Step 4: The 1-Click Export
Once your B-roll is layered over the audio, use Descript's "Social Clips" feature to automatically identify high-impact moments and export them as vertical videos for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
