## Introduction
You don't need a farm or a basement to grow fresh, gourmet mushrooms. With modern grow boxes and a bit of know-how, even the smallest apartment can yield a bountiful harvest.

## Choosing the Right Mushroom Species
- **Oyster Mushrooms:** Easiest for beginners, fast growth, minimal odor.
- **Shiitake:** Requires more patience but offers superior flavor.
- **Lion's Mane:** Prized for its texture and potential cognitive benefits.

## Essential Equipment for Apartment Growing
- **Mushroom Grow Box/Kit:** Pre-inoculated substrate blocks are the easiest start.
- **Humidity Control:** A simple humidifier with a timer or ultrasonic fogger.
- **Fresh Air Exchange (FAE):** A small USB fan on a timer prevents CO2 buildup.
- **Lighting:** Low-level LED grow lights (mushrooms don't need intense light).

## Step-by-Step Apartment Setup
1. Choose a spot away from direct sunlight and drafts (a closet or shelf works perfectly).
2. Set up your grow box following the manufacturer's instructions.
3. Maintain humidity at 80-90% using your humidifier.
4. Run FAE fan for 5-10 minutes every 4-6 hours.
5. Harvest your first flush in 10-14 days.
